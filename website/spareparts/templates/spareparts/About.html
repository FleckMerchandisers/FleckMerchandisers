About

What are we?


FleckMerchandisers provides a platform for the exchange of spare parts to the people who need it. Considering that certain parts can be replaced, which involve a certain amount of repair, we are developing a platform where the user can search for the spare parts for his/her requirements. Based on the Demand list generated by the supplier, the exchange of product is facilitated.


Objective

We support and work in hand for the exchange to spare parts and think of a better future.

  • Reduce the impact in the environment
  • Save money
  • Support local green businesses

How


To minimize your operational interruptions, FleckMerchandisers platform offers an extended range of parts for exchange, such as rudders, elevators, winglets or sharklets, before or after the removal of your part. Exchange of spare parts, Planned Obsolesce, policy of producing consumer goods that rapidly become obsolete and so require replacing, achieved by frequent changes in design, termination of the supply of spare parts, and the use of non-durable materials.
